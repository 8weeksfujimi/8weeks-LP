Our 8-Week Journey to This Place

In 2020, we moved from the city with our one-year-old daughter
and fell in love with Fujimi-machi, making the decision to relocate within 8 weeks.

Spending luxurious time surrounded by rich nature,
by the end of 8 weeks, we found ourselves thinking "we want to continue living in this town."

We named it "8weeks" with the hope that our guests would also experience
that feeling of "wanting to stay here."

Day Trips to Surrounding Areas

Hara Village・Tateshina 20-30 min drive
  • Tateshina Kogen Barakura English Garden - Tea time in authentic English garden
  • Yatsugatake Alpaca Farm - Interactive experiences with adorable alpacas
  • Hara Village Star Festival Venue - One of Japan's best stargazing spots
  • Tateshina Teddy Bear Museum - World teddy bear collection
  • Kurumayama Kogen - Nikko-kisuge flowers in summer, ski resort in winter
Suwa 30 min drive
  • Suwa Taisha Upper & Lower Shrines - Prayers at one of Japan's oldest shrine complexes
  • Lake Suwa - Lakeside walks and Kamisuwa hot spring town exploration
  • Suwa Lake Geyser Center - Experience the power of nature
  • Katakura-kan - Showa retro public bath for 1000 people
Kobuchizawa 30 min drive
  • Kobuchizawa Highland Cafe Tour - Hidden gems in the southern foothills of Yatsugatake
  • Risonare Kobuchizawa - Elegant moments at highland resort
  • Craft shops around Kobuchizawa Station - Discover works by local artists
Hakushu・Nirasaki 30-40 min drive
  • Suntory Hakushu Distillery - Whisky factory tour at the forest distillery
  • Ojira River Valley - Crystal clear streams and mystical waterfalls
  • Nirasaki Wineries - Kai region sake and wine tasting
  • Chateraise Hakushu Factory - Ice cream factory tour and tasting
  • Shosenkyo Gorge - One of Japan's premier gorges and crystal village
